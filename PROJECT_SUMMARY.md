## FILE STRUCTURE

src/
├── context/
│   ├── ProductContext.js      (8 products, search, filter)
│   ├── CartContext.js         (cart management, localStorage)
│   ├── WishlistContext.js     (favorites, persistence)
│   ├── UserContext.js         (auth, profile, order history)
│   ├── NotificationContext.js (toast notifications)
│   └── DiscountContext.js     (coupon management)
├── components/
│   ├── Header.js              (navigation, user menu)
│   ├── Footer.js              (site footer)
│   ├── ProductCard.js         (product grid card with quick view)
│   ├── NotificationStack.js   (toast display)
│   ├── QuickViewModal.js      (product preview dialog)
│   └── TestimonialsSection.js (customer reviews)
├── pages/
│   ├── Home.js                (hero, features, products, testimonials, newsletter)
│   ├── Products.js            (browse with filters & sorting)
│   ├── ProductDetail.js       (full product info)
│   ├── Cart.js                (view/edit cart)
│   ├── Checkout.js            (3-step checkout with coupons)
│   ├── OrderConfirmation.js   (order success)
│   ├── Login.js               (login/signup)
│   ├── Wishlist.js            (favorite items)
│   └── Account.js             (user profile & orders)
├── utils/
│   └── formatCurrency.js      (KES currency formatter)
├── App.js                     (routing & provider setup)
└── App.css                    (global styles)

## TECHNOLOGY STACK

- **React 18** (via Create React App)
- **Material-UI v5** (all UI components)
- **React Router v6** (client-side routing)
- **React Context API** (state management)
- **localStorage API** (data persistence)
- **Unsplash API** (product images)
- **Avataaars API** (customer avatars)
